# Weekly Premium Pricing Model

GigGuard uses a dynamic weekly pricing model designed for gig workers who operate on short income cycles.

Premium Formula

weekly_premium = base_rate × zone_multiplier × weather_multiplier × history_multiplier

Components

Base Rate
The base premium rate is ₹35 per week.

Zone Multiplier
Each delivery zone has different environmental risk levels. Zones with frequent flooding or high pollution receive higher multipliers.

Weather Multiplier
Weather forecasts influence the risk level for the upcoming week.

History Multiplier
Workers with frequent past claims may have slightly higher premiums.

Coverage Amount

coverage_amount = avg_daily_earning × disruption_hours × 0.8

GigGuard replaces approximately 80% of lost income during disruption events.

Example

Daily earnings: ₹700
Disruption hours: 6

Payout ≈ ₹420
