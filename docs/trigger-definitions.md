# Parametric Trigger Definitions

GigGuard relies on objective environmental signals rather than manual claims.

Heavy Rain
Trigger threshold: rainfall greater than 15 mm per hour.

Severe AQI
Trigger threshold: air quality index above 300.

Extreme Heat
Trigger threshold: temperature above 44°C.

Flood Alerts
Triggered when city authorities issue flood warnings.

Curfew or Strike
Triggered when city-wide curfew or strikes restrict movement.

Trigger Process

Environmental data is continuously monitored. If thresholds are exceeded, the system estimates lost working hours and automatically generates claims for affected workers.

This ensures workers receive payouts quickly without filing manual requests.
